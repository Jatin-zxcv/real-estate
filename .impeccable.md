# Sharma Real Estates Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Discerning homebuyers, investors, and commercial clients seeking premium properties in Hisar, Haryana. Users arrive with intent — researching properties that align with their vision for sophisticated living or sound investments. The job to be done: find a trusted real estate partner who understands value, quality, and the emotional significance of finding the right property. Users value trust and expertise over flashy marketing and expect the website to reflect the company's commitment to building lasting assets.

### Brand Personality
**Three words:** Trusted, Premium, Grounded

**Voice & Tone:** Quiet confidence. Copy is sparse, purposeful, and intentional — never salesy or pushy. Headlines use evocative, aspirational language ("We don't just build properties — we build assets"). The tone conveys expertise and reliability without urgency.

**Emotional Goals:** Evoke a sense of trust, investment security, and the feeling of being guided by experts who genuinely care about finding the right property for each client.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Visual Tone:** Quiet luxury, warm sophistication, modern minimalism. The aesthetic should feel like a premium property showroom — every element intentional, nothing gratuitous. Properties should be showcased with gallery-like reverence.

**Color System:**
- `--base-100: rgb(242, 237, 230)` — Warm cream (primary light)
- `--base-200: rgb(204, 200, 194)` — Muted stone
- `--base-250: rgb(179, 167, 152)` — Warm taupe
- `--base-300: rgb(153, 143, 130)` — Muted earth
- `--base-350: rgb(128, 119, 108)` — Grounded gray
- `--base-400: rgb(102, 95, 86)` — Deep stone
- `--base-450: rgb(31, 29, 29)` — Near-black warm
- `--base-500: rgb(20, 19, 19)` — Primary dark background

**Typography:**
- **Primary:** Manrope (200-800 weights) — clean, geometric sans-serif with warmth
- **Accent:** DM Mono — uppercase, used for labels and callouts
- **Scale:** 5rem/3.5rem/2.5rem/1.25rem/1rem with tight letter-spacing (-0.25rem to -0.075rem)
- **Line heights:** 1.05-1.4 (tight for headlines, relaxed for body)

**Theme:** Dark mode primary (`--base-500` backgrounds), with warm cream accents. The palette evokes natural materials — raw plaster, aged stone, soft leather.

**References (inferred):**
- Premium real estate platforms with editorial quality
- Luxury property developers with immersive showcases
- Investment firms with understated confidence

**Anti-references (avoid):**
- Bright, saturated colors or gradients
- Busy layouts with competing elements
- Generic real estate stock imagery
- Aggressive CTAs or promotional language
- "URGENT" or "LIMITED TIME" messaging
- Trendy effects that feel gimmicky rather than purposeful

### Design Principles

1. **Restraint as strength** — Every element must earn its place. When in doubt, remove. White space (or dark space) is as important as content.

2. **Motion with purpose** — Animations (GSAP) should feel organic and architectural — smooth reveals, subtle parallax, gentle easing. The custom "hop" ease (0.9, 0, 0.1, 1) creates confident, weighted transitions. Never add motion for decoration.

3. **Typography as texture** — Headlines are large, tightly tracked, and weighted. Body copy is sparse and carefully set. The contrast between Manrope and DM Mono creates visual rhythm without color.

4. **Warmth in darkness** — The dark UI feels inviting, not cold. Warm grays, soft cream accents, and subtle transparency (rgba overlays, backdrop blur) create depth and softness.

5. **Consistent spatial rhythm** — Use the established spacing system: 2rem container padding, 1rem gaps, generous section margins. Border radii range from 0.35rem (buttons) to 2rem (cards/panels).

### Component Patterns

- **Containers:** 2rem padding, max-width 2000px, centered
- **Cards/Panels:** Background `rgba(242, 237, 230, 0.1)` with `backdrop-filter: blur(1rem)`, 1rem border-radius
- **Buttons:** Pill-shaped (4rem border-radius), outlined or filled, animated on hover
- **Tags:** Outlined pills with `--base-400` borders
- **Images:** Full bleed with `object-fit: cover`, subtle border-radius (0.75rem-2rem)
- **Sections:** Full viewport width, generous vertical padding (10rem+ between major sections)

### Animation Library

- **Scroll-triggered reveals:** Elements animate in on scroll using ScrollTrigger
- **Text animations:** Split-type for character/word reveals
- **Smooth scroll:** Lenis for buttery scroll experience
- **View transitions:** Next.js view transitions for page navigation
- **Hover states:** 0.3s ease transitions on color/opacity

### Accessibility Considerations

- Maintain minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for text on dark backgrounds
- `--base-200` on `--base-500` should be the minimum for body text
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for users who need it
- Ensure interactive elements have clear focus states

## Website Behavior & Microinteraction Inventory

### Routes
- `/`: preloader, hero, stats, property tags reveal, featured properties stack, client testimonials carousel, gallery callout, CTA.
- `/about`: hero copy, facts strip, HowWeWork sticky process, CTA, Spotlight pinned sequence.
- `/properties`: animated property list with transition navigation and category filters.
- `/properties/[id]`: property detail layout with staged copy reveals, image gallery, and inquiry form.
- `/blueprints`: draggable infinite gallery with expand/collapse detail state.
- `/blog`: blog listing with article cards and category filters.
- `/contact`: staged contact information, inquiry form, and hero image.

### Global Interaction System
- Navigation uses `navigateWithTransition()` (`src/hooks/useViewTransition.js`) with 2s old/new root transition.
- App wrapper (`src/client-layout.js`) applies Lenis smooth-scroll settings by viewport width.
- `globals.css` menu lock: all pointer events disabled during menu-open except menu and toggle.
- Global `user-select: none` on all elements.

### Nav + Menu
- State machine: `isOpen`, `isAnimating`, `isNavigating`, init guard.
- Open:
  - menu clip-path `circle(0 -> 100%)`, `2s`, `power3.out`
  - SplitText lines (`h2`, `p`) animate `y:120% -> 0%`, staggered/delayed
  - body gets `menu-open`.
- Close:
  - text lines `y:0 -> -120%`
  - menu clip-path closes `1s` after `0.75s` delay
  - lines reset, pointer events restored, navigation guard reset.
- Link behavior:
  - same-route click closes menu only
  - other routes navigate with transition.
- Toggle button:
  - frosted capsule (`blur(10px)`)
  - hamburger morph to X (`250ms`) in opened state.

### Top Bar
- Scroll-down past 50px: hide (`y:-height`, `1s`, `power4.out`).
- Scroll-up: show (`y:0`, `1s`).
- Throttle via 100ms timeout gate.
- Logo and reserve CTA route through view transition hook.

### Copy + AnimatedButton
- `Copy`:
  - waits for fonts
  - SplitText lines from `y:100%` to `y:0%`, `1s`, stagger `0.1`, `power4.out`
  - optional scroll trigger (`top 90%`, once).
- `AnimatedButton`:
  - mount timeline: button scale pop, circle/icon reveal, text line reveal
  - hover: circle expands to full width, icon shifts right, text color inverts
  - click intercept -> transition navigation.

### Home Microinteractions
- Preloader:
  - counter digits in/out sequence
  - spinner fade
  - logo words in/out
  - divider `scaleY(0 -> 100%)`
  - block clip-path reveal
  - completion unlocks interaction and restarts Lenis.
- What-we-do tags:
  - one-time reveal: `opacity + x` staggered entry.

### Featured Projects
- Per-card 3D scroll choreography:
  - `y`, `z`, `rotationX` shifts as next card enters
  - pinned stack behavior with scrubbed progress
  - overlay depth cue via `--after-opacity`.

### Client Reviews
- Initial: first tab expands, first info and review reveal.
- Click tab:
  - old info fades out
  - widths swap (active contracts, target expands)
  - review text lines animate out/in
  - image crossfades via injected image node.
- Hover: tab background transitions (`500ms`).

### How We Work
- Step chips reveal once with horizontal slide.
- Desktop:
  - header pinned while cards scroll
  - active step updates on enter/leave callbacks.
- Active step UI:
  - width `4rem -> 8rem`
  - label fades/translates in
  - index shifts right.

### Spotlight
- Pinned long-scroll sequence (`+10 viewport heights`, scrub).
- Phase 1: intro words move apart, background scales in.
- Phase 2: intro fades, title rails/header appear.
- Phase 3:
  - title track scrolls upward
  - thumbnails move on bezier arc (`gap`, `speed`, `arcRadius`)
  - nearest title highlighted; background image swaps to active.
- Phase 4: header/rails fade out.

### Spaces
- Cards initialize hidden (`opacity 0`, `scale 0.75`, `y 150`).
- First card reveals after delay.
- Remaining cards reveal on viewport entry.
- Card click triggers transition navigation.
- Filter chips currently decorative (no filtering logic).

### Blueprints Gallery
- Drag engine:
  - mouse/touch update target offsets
  - RAF interpolation with ease factor `0.085`
  - release momentum when velocity threshold exceeded.
- Virtualized grid:
  - directional buffered visibility window
  - dynamic item create/remove.
- Intro: random staggered item scale-in.
- Expand flow:
  - clicked item hidden
  - cloned fixed item appended to body
  - other items fade out
  - clone animates to centered large size (`1s`, `hop` ease)
  - title words animate in.
- Close flow:
  - title words out
  - clone animates back to source rect and is removed
  - source item restored, grid fades in, drag re-enabled.
- Cleanup runs on unmount, visibilitychange, and beforeunload.

### Footer
- Hidden on `/blueprints`.
- Nav links use transition navigation.
- Social icons reveal on scroll (`opacity/x` staggered).

### Motion Tokens
- Common eases: `power2.out`, `power3.out`, `power4.out`, `power4.in`, `back.out(1.7)`.
- Custom ease: `hop` (`0.9,0,0.1,1`).
- Typical duration bands:
  - `0.3s` micro fades
  - `0.5s` hover/sub-animations
  - `1s+` narrative transitions
  - `2s` route/menu envelope animations.

### Responsive Interaction Notes
- Main breakpoint: `max-width: 1000px`; secondary at `1200px` (client reviews).
- Lenis config changes at mobile breakpoint.
- Spotlight title rails hidden on mobile.
- Multi-column sections collapse to stacked layouts.

### Gaps / Non-Wired Interaction Areas
- Menu/contact social labels are text only (not links).
- Space filters are presentational only.
- No explicit `prefers-reduced-motion` branch implemented.
